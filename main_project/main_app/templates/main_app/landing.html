About Us

A portal to inter-connect citizens and hospitals
For hospitals:
The portal connects every other hospital to suffice their logistical needs such as lack of oxygen cylinders,beds etc.
The portal also facilitates proper interaction between hospitals by keeping proper track of each hospital's requests(asked/received).Each hospital can look at the status of every other hospital and can accordingly request the hospitals who are in a condition to donate.
For citizens:
The portal also predicts the percentage wise probablity of the user being infected with COVID-19 Users can also keep track of the hospitals and add them to the watchlist so that they can keep a track of the hospitals easily.
